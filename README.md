About HostelMart
HostelMart is a campus-only second-hand marketplace designed exclusively for hostel and college students. It is a platform where students can list items they no longer need and discover affordable second-hand goods posted by their fellow campus mates — all in one organised, searchable, and trusted space.
Whether you are a final-year student looking to sell your old textbooks before you graduate, or a first-year student trying to furnish your hostel room on a tight budget, HostelMart connects you with the right people — right on your campus.

HostelMart is not just another buy-sell app. It is a community-first platform built around the trust that already exists between campus neighbours.


The Problem We Are Solving
Every semester, students across college hostels face the same situation:

Unused textbooks, electronics, cycles, and furniture pile up after each year.
General platforms like OLX or Facebook Marketplace connect students with strangers — not trusted peers.
WhatsApp groups and notice boards are chaotic, have no search, and listings disappear quickly.
Incoming students overpay for brand-new items when perfectly good second-hand alternatives exist just a few doors away.

There is no dedicated, organised, and trusted platform built specifically for campus-level peer-to-peer commerce. HostelMart fills that gap.

What You Can Do on HostelMart
Browse & Discover

Explore all active listings on a clean, card-based home page.
Each listing shows the item image, title, price, category, and condition at a glance.
No account required to browse — anyone can discover items.

Search, Filter & Sort

Search for any item by keyword — results appear as you type.
Filter by category: Books, Electronics, Furniture, Clothing, Sports, and more.
Filter by price range to stay within your budget.
Filter by condition: New, Like New, Good, or Fair.
Sort by Newest First, Price: Low to High, or Price: High to Low.

Sell an Item

Create your free account and log in.
Post a listing with a title, description, price, category, condition, and photo.
Your listing goes live instantly and is visible to the entire campus.

Manage Your Listings

Visit your personal Dashboard to see all your active listings in one place.
Edit any listing to update the price, description, or details.
Mark an item as Sold once it has been picked up.
Delete a listing whenever you want.

Your Profile

View and update your profile information.
Switch between Dark Mode and Light Mode — your preference is remembered.

How to Use HostelMart
As a Buyer

Open HostelMart — no login needed to browse.
Use the search bar or filters to find what you need.
Click on any item to see full details and seller information.
Contact the seller directly to arrange pickup on campus.

As a Seller

Create your free account using your name and email.
Click Post Item and fill in your item details.
Your listing is live immediately — visible to all campus users.
Manage everything from your Dashboard: edit, mark as sold, or delete.
